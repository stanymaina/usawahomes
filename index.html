    About Us

    The creation of the company Usawa Homes Ltd comes from the desire to facilitate universities with good student accommodation and to serve the buyer with a strong return on investment.
    The company targets to be the Number One provider of student accommodation across Africa. The Company has stemmed out of an investment group Usawa Investments Ltd as their property portfolio company. Current project underway is Lukenya Hills Suite’s.
    Usawa Homes Ltd is a real estate company founded by the Usawa Investment Group in 2014 with Joel Chepwony as the chairperson. It arose from the desire to offer conducive student accommodation and also offer investment opportunities with low financial entry requirements to individuals interested in real estate. With the sharp growth in the education sector after the introduction of free primary education in 2003 and with free secondary education to be introduced in 2018, enrollment of learners in universities continues to increase rapidly. This increase has led to the shortage of accommodation in both public and private universities in Kenya today.
    It is this scarcity that Usawa Homes Ltd is filling by constructing, selling and managing modern and affordable student accommodation. We target to be the best option for investors particularly of student accommodation across the region.
    Our concept relies on the simple logic that almost everybody wishes to have an income stream and rental income is one of the guaranteed ways. At Usawa, we have taken the investment route which ensures the demand for our project by both the investors and tenants is high and there is guaranteed return on investment within 10 years. Lukenya Hills Furnished Suites is the company’s flagship project. It is located 500M from Daystar University Athi River campus.
    Students are often considered to be difficult tenants because of the potential for rental default. Our research shows that landlord with students’ tenants are usually enjoying a good relationship because they pay in advance. And if there is ever a problem, there’s the parental guarantor to call on. Students can also be long term tenants since it takes several years for one to complete a degree. If they are kept happy, they can be tenants throughout their studies. Currently we are the only company in Kenya fully dealing in student accommodation. However they are numerous privately owned hostels spread all over the country. Some of this privately owned hostels may or may not be student friendly. Safety is a big concern for a lot of parents/guardians and their children in colleges. Usawa Homes not only are we targeting to be the best option for investors but also for students and parents seeking accommodations to live in/their children to live in.
